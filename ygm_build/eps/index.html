Earful of Cocktail: Episode 1 (0:00:00 - 0:05:00)
The Cocktail Gang gets "Wild Again" as we set off on a new podtail... or should we say "pod tale"! After all, we have a Whale of a Story to share with you: the story of brash, young, scared military man Brian Flanagan and his dreams of owning the city of New York, one spinning bottle of liquor at a time. New features are debuted: "Drink of the Ep", "Flair in the Air", and "Who Let the Spoilers Out?". And as always, we attempt to answer the question, "what is theirs and what is mine?" The answer may surprise you.

Special note: The makers of You've Got Mail hope you'll visit these sites and help support children's literacy:
  • First Book -- an organization which works with existing literacy programs to distribute new books to children who, for economic or other reasons, have little or no access to books.
  • Pencil -- established in 1995 to galvanize and coordinate corporate and community involvement in New York City public education.
  • Reading Is Fundamental -- RIF is America's leading nonprofit children's literacy organization.

You might not know what ASCII art is, but you've definitely seen it. You know, those pictures people put at the end of their e-mails? Big Tweety Birds® and other fun stuff.

We're taking submissions via email (how else?) for an ASCII art gallery, and we'll post some of the more interesting or creative ones you send to us right here on the You've Got Mail site.

(Three important things -- one, we won't pay you anything, two, we'll only use the art on this site, and three, we'll attach your name to it only if you ask us to.)

Send your submission to gotmail@wbny.com!
